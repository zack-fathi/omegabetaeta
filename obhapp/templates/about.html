About Omega Beta Eta

Our Mission

Omega Beta Eta is committed to creating a support system for Arab men on campus, fostering a community of student leaders. We strive to provide academic support for our Brothers, as well as establishing resources for success in every aspect of life.

Our Vision

Our mission is to offer opportunities for leadership and personal development for our brothers and the Arab community on campus, empowering our community as a whole.

Our Values

Academic Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in education

Brotherhood

Building lifelong bonds and mutual support

Service

Giving back to the community through philanthropy

Our History

Founded in 2018 at the University of Michigan, Omega Beta Eta was established to create a space that celebrates Arab culture while fostering leadership, academic excellence, and lifelong friendships. Through cultural events, academic initiatives, and philanthropy, our fraternity continues to leave a lasting impact on campus and beyond.
