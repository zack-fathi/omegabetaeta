About Omega Beta Eta

Omega Beta Eta is committed to creating a support system for Arab men on campus, fostering a community of student leaders.

We strive to provide academic support for our Brothers, as well as establishing resources for success in every aspect of life.

Our mission is to offer opportunities for leadership and personal development for our brothers and the Arab community on campus, empowering our community as a whole.
